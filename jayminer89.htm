Jay Miner at MetCom 89

The MCCC held a series of events that we titled MetCom. Many, including MetCom '89, were produced with the cooperation of SCOPE, a Dallas user group. For many of these events we had a featured speaker in addition to special demonstrations and classes held in various meeting spaces. Most were held, as was this one, at the Arlington Convention Center, Arlington Texas. Arlington is well known as the home of the Dallas Cowboys as well as the Texas Rangers baseball team and the original Six Flags amusement park.

For MetCom '89 our featured speaker was Jay Miner. The video below includes the entire talk and follow-up question and answer session. The video was produced by Jack Detore who is seen introducing Jay. Run time is just under 50 minutes.

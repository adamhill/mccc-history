Metcom 88 Promo

This is a promotional tape for Metcom 88 put together by our semi-official videographer at the time: Jack Detore. It contains video excerpts from Metcom 88. The quality is not up to today's standards, made worse by the fact that the video tape this was pulled from was 20+ years old at the time this video was created.

Our club-sponsored Metcom events, although primarily local, used to pull in hundreds, and there were usually multiple demonstrations and tutorials going on in various rooms simultaneously.

## *** CORRECTIONS/CHANGES TO CHECKBOOK BALANCER PROGRAM ***

Please make the following correctións to Steve Stockdale's CHECKBOOK BALANCER program which appeared in last month's newsletter:

- 140V(5):V(1)-v(3)+V(2)-V(4)+4(6)
- 145 IF LEN(V$(X))=CT THEN 147
- 255 PRINT:PRINT:PRINT TAB (9) CHR$ (18) " NEW BALANCE ==&gt; $ "V(·5) +V (7)

For you VIC-20 volks, Bill Aiken reports that you can make the following changes to the same program and make it work on your VIC.

```basic
5 PRINI "CLEAR HOME": FOR X=1 TO 44:PRINT "$";: NEXT: PRINT
10 PRINT SPC (2) "CHECKBOOK BALANCER": PRINT
15 FOR X=1 TO 44: PRINT "S";: NEXT
144 V$=LEFT$ (VS,X-1)+STR$(V(X))+MID$ (VS,X+L): CT=I: TB (X) =35
145 IF LEN(V$) =CT THEN 147
220 FOR X=1 TO 44:PRINT "*";: NEXT
260 FOR X=1 TO 44:PRINT "*";: NEXT
```

Thanks, Bill!
